CABINS

All cabin bookings MUST occur from Friday to Sunday

Pukeko Cabins

Tucked away in a remote corner of the Mountain Alps, The Pukeko Cabins are a classic ski log cabin exuding timeless mountain elegance. Crafted from locally sourced timber, this two-story retreat boasts a sprawling stone fireplace, exposed wooden beams, and breathtaking panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks from its oversized windows. Inside, rich leather furnishings and fur throws add a touch of luxury to the rustic charm, while a private sauna offers post-ski relaxation. The wraparound deck provides a front-row seat to the pristine slopes, inviting you to bask in the beauty of the Swiss winter wonderland.

  • Maximum 15 Children and 6 Adults
  • Optimal for large groups/families wanting a private space

Keruru Cabins

Perched high in the Rocky Mountains, The Keruru Cabins is a spacious and luxurious ski log cabin that epitomizes mountain grandeur. Constructed from massive logs and stone, this multi-level retreat offers commanding views of rugged peaks through its towering windows. The interior is a blend of rustic elegance and contemporary design, featuring a grand stone fireplace, a gourmet kitchen, and sumptuous leather sofas. With a private helipad and direct access to some of the region's most challenging slopes, it caters to the adventurous skier seeking both comfort and adrenaline-pumping thrills.

  • Maximum 30 Children and 10 Adults
  • The largest out of all cabins

Kakapo Cabins

Step inside Kakapo Cabin, and you'll find a warm and inviting interior adorned with Maori-inspired artwork and earthy tones. The open-plan living space features a roaring wood-burning fireplace, perfect for warming up after a day on the nearby ski trails. The kitchen, equipped with modern amenities, encourages gatherings over hearty meals inspired by local cuisine. Each bedroom is a snug retreat, offering a peaceful night's sleep under the soft glow of custom-made wooden lamps.

  • Maximum 2 Children and 4 Adults
  • Greatest option for smaller families looking for time away
